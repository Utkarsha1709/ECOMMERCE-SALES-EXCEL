## Project Overview
The goal of this project is to analyze and visualize E-commerce sales performance using Microsoft Excel to understand the company’s sales distribution, profit trends, and customer purchasing behavior.
The analysis helps identify high-performing products, regions, and customer segments, enabling data-driven decisions to improve business growth and profitability.
________________________________________
## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transaction-level information, including:
•	Order ID: Unique identifier for each order.
•	Order Date / Ship Date: Dates representing when the order was placed and shipped.
•	Customer Name: Name of the customer who made the purchase.
•	Segment: Category of customer (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office).
•	Region / State / City: Geographical data showing where sales occurred.
•	Category / Sub-Category: Product classification such as Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.
•	Sales: Revenue generated from the order.
•	Quantity: Number of units sold.
•	Discount: Discount applied on the order.
•	Profit: Profit gained from the sale.
________________________________________
## Business Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:
•	To identify top-selling products, categories, and sub-categories.
•	To analyze regional and state-wise sales and profit distribution.
•	To understand how discounts affect overall profit.
•	To build an interactive Excel dashboard to support managerial decision-making and strategy planning.
________________________________________
## Key Business Questions
Below are the key questions explored in this project:
•	Which product categories generate the highest revenue and profit?
•	Which states or regions perform best in terms of sales and profitability?
•	How do discounts influence profit margins?
•	Which customer segment contributes most to total revenue?
•	What are the monthly sales and profit trends over time?

## Insights (Sample Findings)
1. Category Performance:
•	Technology products generated the highest sales and profit.
•	Office Supplies had the highest sales volume but lower profit margins due to higher discounts.
2. Regional & State Trends:
•	The West and East regions showed the best performance in both sales and profit.
•	California and New York were the top-performing states.
3. Segment Analysis:
•	The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
•	Corporate customers maintained steady profit growth.
4. Discount Impact:
•	Higher discounts led to decreased profit margins, showing that optimizing discount strategies is crucial.
5. Monthly Sales Trend:
•	Sales showed seasonal peaks during the year-end period, suggesting strong festival or holiday-related demand.
